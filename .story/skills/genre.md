# Skill: Genre

## Purpose

Maintain consistent genre expectations and emotional tone.

## Mandatory Rules

- Primary genre: grounded science fiction
- Tone: intimate, tense, slightly melancholic
- Stakes are personal before they are global

## Prohibited Patterns

- Comedy during high-stakes scenes
- Deus ex machina solutions
- Genre blending without setup

## Examples

### ❌ Disallowed

"The console exploded, but somehow everything worked out."

### ✅ Allowed

"The console failed. What survived was smaller—and worse."

## Validation

- [ ] Genre remains consistent
- [ ] Tension preserved
